About Me

My name is Billy Dorman and I am many things. First and foremost, I am a father to an amazing 3 year old daughter... Adalynn. And the husband to my better half, Lauren.

Being a Web Developer has opened a lot of doors for me and given me back my life and creativity. Learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript has enabled me showcase my creative talents, make great money, and work independently.
